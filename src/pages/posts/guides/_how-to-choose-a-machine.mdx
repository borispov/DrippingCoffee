### The Last Advice You'll Need
I have researched so much about espresso machines from a point of view of a hobbyist, that I have decided to save you time and 
share fundamental information about the most important machines in the market. You'll see a bunch of popular machines as well as some least known.
However, this isn't about Vintaga or restoration machines, but rather about more modern machines.

This isn't an advice on picking the ultimate machine, but rather giving you important data to help you decide for yourself.
We do not conclude big things unless tt is necessarily urgent.
